Our story

We help young people
dream bigger.

Classrooms to Careers is a youth-led social enterprise built on a simple belief: talent is everywhere, but opportunity isn’t. We close that gap with practical, confidence-building experiences offered completely free of cost.

We believe a young person’s future shouldn’t depend on their zip code, their network, or luck. It should depend on support, curiosity, and a chance to try.

What guides everything we do

◇

Start with curiosity

Learning sticks when it starts with a question. We meet students where their interest lives and grow from there.

♧

Everyone belongs

Our programs are free, welcoming, and built so that every young person feels they have a place at the table.

Build for the real world

How we got here

July 10, 2025

The idea is born

CTC is founded with a single belief: students deserve to understand who they are before they're asked to choose what they'll become.

July 10–20, 2025

Building the blueprint

The team creates a detailed, sustainable operational plan covering curriculum design, outreach strategy, and long-term vision for the program.

July 20 to August 10, 2025

Going all in

The team reaches out to over 50 elementary and middle school counselors and connects with over 30 professionals across various industries.

August–November 2025

Building everything from scratch

Every piece of content is created in-house: games, activities, slideshows, and lesson plans. The team rehearses, tests, collects feedback, and refines.

November 2025

Thanksgiving workshop

CTC executes its first 5-day workshop, reaching over 100 students with 20 students per session, every single day.

February 2026

DECA ICDC qualification

CTC qualifies for the International Career Development Conference, validating the program on a national competitive stage.

April 2026

ICDC

The team competes at ICDC, representing CTC and everything it stands for on one of DECA's biggest stages.

June 1, 2026

Planning a new model

CTC begins designing a bold new program model: broader, deeper, and built to scale across more schools, students, and cities.

June 20, 2026

Expanding the network

CTC begins reaching out to school counselors, city council members, and university professors to build institutional partnerships.
